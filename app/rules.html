The Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge

How the Challenge Works

For ten (10) weeks, the nation’s top coaches will be counting on fans like you to vote for their favorite charity. Vote for your coach’s favorite charity. Help increase their chances by sharing with your friends. The coaches receiving the most votes gain a spot in the finals, with the winning coach receiving $100,000 toward his charity (each coach will get at least $1,000 for his favorite cause). So start voting now!

About the Voting

Vote for one coach’s charity per day.

  • Round 1 voting runs from 1/4/16 at 3:00PM ET - 1/24/16 at 11:59AM ET.
  • Round 2 voting runs from 1/25/16 at 1:00PM ET - 2/14/16 at 11:59AM ET.
  • Round 3 voting runs from 2/15/16 at 1:00PM ET - 2/28/16 at 11:59AM ET.
  • Final Four voting runs from 2/29/16 at 1:00PM ET - 3/13/16 at 11:59AM ET.

Don’t forget to vote each day.

Visit www.espn.com/infiniti on 3/13/2016 after voting is closed for the winner announcement.

How to Vote

Between 3:00 PM Eastern Time (“ET”) on 1/4/16 and 11:59 AM ET on 1/24/16 (“Voting Period”), forty-eight (48) college basketball Coaches (each a “Coach” and collectively “Coaches”) along with their designated charity (each a “Designated Charity” and collectively “Designated Charities”) will be profiled on www.espn.com, keyword “Infiniti.” To vote for a college basketball Coach and his Designated Charity visit www.espn.com, search for keyword “Infiniti,” complete the ESPN.com member sign-in and follow the instructions provided to complete and submit the voting form. The voting will be determined by Sponsor in its sole and absolute discretion. There is a limit of one (1) vote per person, per e-mail address, per ESPN registrant, per day (24-hours from 12:00 AM EST) for the duration of the Voting Period. Additional votes received beyond the specified limit or votes generated by script, macro or other automated means or with the intent to affect the integrity of the voting process will be void. If a participant votes by accessing the ESPN mobile site, message and data rates may apply, depending on participant’s individual wireless pricing plan, in accordance with his/her wireless customer agreement. Check carrier for details. Mobile Web service may not be available in all areas. The twenty-four (24) Coaches and their Designated Charities that receive the most votes during the Voting Period will be eligible for the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 2 Voting Period.

Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 2 Voting Period

The twenty four (24) Coaches and their Designated Charities that receive the most votes throughout the duration of the Voting Period, as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion, will become Round 2 Coaches and will be profiled on www.espn.com, keyword “Infiniti.” Eligible Voters can vote for the Round 2 Coaches between 1:00 PM ET on 1/25/16 and 11:59 AM ET on 2/14/16 (“Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 2 Voting Period”). Voting is open to Eligible Voters who are eighteen (18) years of age or older as of 1/25/16. There is a limit of one (1) vote per person, per e-mail address, per ESPN registrant, per day for the duration of the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 2 Voting Period. Additional votes received beyond the specified limit or votes generated by script, macro or other automated means or with the intent to affect the integrity of the voting process will be void.

Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 3 Voting Period

The sixteen (16) Coaches and their Designated Charities that receive the most votes throughout the duration of the Round 2 Voting Period, as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion, will become Round 3 Coaches and will be profiled on www.espn.com, keyword “Infiniti.” Eligible Voters can vote for the Round 3 Coaches between 1:00 PM ET on 2/15/16 and 11:59 AM ET on 2/29/16 (“Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 3 Voting Period”). Voting is open to Eligible Voters who are eighteen (18) years of age or older as of 2/16/16. There is a limit of one (1) vote per person, per e-mail address, per ESPN registrant, per day for the duration of the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Round 3 Voting Period. Additional votes received beyond the specified limit or votes generated by script, macro or other automated means or with the intent to affect the integrity of the voting process will be void.

Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Finalist Voting Period

The four (4) Coaches and their Designated Charities that receive the most votes throughout the duration of the Round 3 Voting Period, as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion, (each a “Finalist”) will be profiled on www.espn.com, keyword “Infiniti.” Eligible Voters can vote for the Finalists between 1:00 PM ET on 2/29/16 and 11:59 AM ET on 3/13/16 (“Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Finalist Voting Period”). Voting is open to Eligible Voters who are eighteen (18) years of age or older as of 2/29/16. There is a limit of one (1) vote per person, per e-mail address, per ESPN registrant, per day for the duration of the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Finalist Voting Period. Additional votes received beyond the specified limit or votes generated by script, macro or other automated means or with the intent to affect the integrity of the voting process will be void.

Winning Coach/Charity Selection

The Finalist Coach and his Designated Charity that receives the most votes throughout the duration of the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Finalist Voting Period (as determined by Sponsor in its sole discretion) will be deemed the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Winner (subject to these Official Terms). In the event of a tie for the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Winner, there will be another day of voting (one 24-hour period) added to the end of the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Finalist Voting Period, when eligible voters can vote for only the tied Finalist Coaches and their Designated Charities and the Finalist Coach/Designated Charity that receives the most votes during this 24-hour period will be determined the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Winner (subject to these Official Terms). The Winning Coach from the 2016 Challenge agrees to forego participation in the following year’s Infiniti Coaches Charity Challenge program, but will be eligible to participate again after sitting out for one year.

Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Donations

The Finalist Coach’s Designated Charity that receives the most votes will be deemed the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge Winner and will receive a donation in the amount of $100,000 (subject to confirmation). The forty-seven (47) remaining Coaches’ Designated Charities will receive a donation in the amount of $1,000 per coach eliminated in Round 1, $7,500 per Coach eliminated in Round 2, $10,000 per Coach eliminated in Round 3, $15,000 per Coach eliminated in Round 4, and $100,000 for the Finalist Coach. Donations will be awarded directly to the Designated Charities, and not to the Coaches. Total value of all donations to be awarded in this Challenge: $349,000.
