Welcome to the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge

As a proud corporate partner of NCAA Men’s Basketball, Infiniti will donate to each participating charity. The coach’s charity with the most votes wins.

How it works:

  • Support your coach by voting daily on ESPN.com/Infiniti.
  • Coaches with the most votes advance to the next round and voting restarts.
  • Infiniti will donate at least $1,000 to each charity, and the winning charity receives $100,000.
  • The winner is announced March 16.

Support your coach by sharing the Infiniti Coaches’ Charity Challenge with friends and family! Read more about the coaches and their charities below.
